Study Game

Contracting Jeopardy

Three full rounds. Twelve categories. Over 300 clues on the Revolutionary FAR Overhaul, source selection, post-award, and the parts of the job that actually come up on the desk. Play solo to drill or hot-seat with coworkers.

FAR references reflect the Revolutionary FAR Overhaul (RFO). Specific section numbers may shift as the overhaul continues.
How to play. Pick a category and dollar value. Read the clue. Reveal the answer. Mark right (score added) or wrong (score subtracted). Finish the board, then on to Double Jeopardy (double values, two Daily Doubles). Finish that, then Final Jeopardy: wager first, clue second. Multi-player takes turns and each player self-scores on the honor system.
