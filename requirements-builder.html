Requirements Package Builder

Meet Ollie. He hosts a family of tools for building out acquisition packages: requirements definition, PWS drafting, market research, sole-source justifications, brand-name justifications. This first preview is the entry to requirements definition.

OLLIE
▼

Early build. Ollie walks you through a product Statement of Requirement and spits out a clean PDF you can hand to CONS. Services get a short intake then hand off to the PWS builder. Your answers ride the belt to the next station (market research, justifications, evaluation factors) so you don't have to retype anything. Click the dialogue box to advance. Nothing here is a sanctioned form — it's a working doc to help you think.

Fill-in Mode

Every question Ollie asks. Fill what applies, leave the rest blank. Sections expand when their trigger answer is picked. When you’re done, “Build Package” at the bottom drops you on the final review screen with the full PDF ready.
