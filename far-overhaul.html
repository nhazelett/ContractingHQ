FAR Overhaul

Revolutionary FAR Overhaul Tracker

Aggregated news and updates from government, practitioner, and legal sources. Updated daily.

What is the RFO? The government is rewriting the rules for how it buys things. The FAR (Federal Acquisition Regulation) has governed federal contracting since 1984. Executive Order 14275 kicked off a full overhaul in April 2025, shifting from rigid, step-by-step rules to broader principles that give contracting officers more room to use their judgment. Changes are rolling out in waves through class deviations, with formal rulemaking still ahead.

This page pulls RFO-related news automatically from government agencies, law firms, and practitioners every day. For the official source of truth, check acquisition.gov/far-overhaul. To compare old vs. new FAR language, use our FAR Comparator.
