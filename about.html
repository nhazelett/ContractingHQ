About KTHQ

Hey guys, I'm Nick! I've been in contracting for about 14 years, and over the years we've been inundated with so much information that my head spins. WarU, JKO, this CO does it THIS way, that CO does it THAT way, FAR changes, policy changes, the RFO comes out, etc. It's ALL good stuff. But after all this time, I think I'm out of brain cells. And then I thought, "How the hell are our new Airmen and 1102's figuring this stuff out while WE'RE still figuring out these rapid changes?" So I figured I'd go back and build something that helps. Training. Step by step, back to the true basics, what is a NAICS code? How do I do market research? What do I need to learn, what does the right way look like, and how do I go about doing it on my own after I learn it? So that's what I built. I plan to expand and simplify additional training in the future!

Supervisors in the Air Force have already been doing this, it's nothing new. But I wanted to build something consistent for newer folks to learn, and then to come back to for a refresher if they need it.

Also we have so many "One Stop Shops" I figured I'd consolidate them all into my links page. Let me know if I missed any!

I do this in my spare time, and I'm hoping to keep updating it with content as much as possible. I've never possessed coding skills, but with the help of Claude AI, I've been able to put experience on a platform, and give it to you all for free. That being said, I'm human, so if you find that I've messed something up, or said the wrong thing, or trained the wrong way, or missed something, please let me know.

I want to keep this page fun and light, so feel free to enjoy my dumb contracting songs, if you have any ideas for improvement, please feel free to hit me up. Also, go see the CMCoE page for fantastic memes.

I plan to keep it free forever, but if you'd like to support the page, I do have a buy me a coffee link up top, but if not, I've got a day job to fall back on, no worries.
