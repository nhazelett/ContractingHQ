About KTHQ

KTHQ — ContractingHQ — is a practitioner-led resource hub built for U.S. government contracting officers. It exists because the good information is scattered, the training is mostly slide decks, and too much of what's out there was written by people who stopped doing the work. This site is the opposite of that: direct, current, and built by someone who is still in it.

The site is built for COs at every stage — from the new warrant holder figuring out their first simplified acquisition to the deployed CCO running contingency contracts in a combat zone. Every tool, summary, and resource on this site is designed to cut through the noise and get you to the answer you actually need. No fluff. No paywall. No login required.

KTHQ is built and maintained by an active federal contracting professional. The mission is simple: make the resources that should exist, available to the people who need them. It will stay free, forever.
